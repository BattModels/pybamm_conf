### Training Session

The hands-on training session will be conducted by [Ionworks](https://ionworks.com/) and will cover battery modeling with PyBaMM, including how to build and run physics-based battery models directly in the open-source library, from model setup and parameterization to simulation and analysis. We will also cover how to use AI agents to accelerate PyBaMM workflows, from writing simulation code to interpreting results. The session is suitable for researchers and engineers who are new to PyBaMM, as well as those looking to work faster. Training will run on both days of the conference from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM.
