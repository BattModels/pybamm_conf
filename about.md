## About PyBaMM

[PyBaMM (Python Battery Mathematical Modelling)](https://pybamm.org/) is an open-source battery simulation package in Python. The mission is to accelerate battery modelling research through open tools for multi-institutional and interdisciplinary collaboration.

PyBaMM provides:

- A framework for defining and solving differential-equation systems.
- A library of battery models and parameter sets.
- Tools for battery experiment simulation and result visualization.
